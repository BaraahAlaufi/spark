Fit for F1: why nutrition is so important for F1 drivers

Maintaining peak physical condition is crucial, along with strength, endurance, nutrition, and hydration. F1 trainer Mark Arnall emphasizes the importance of understanding how a driver burns fuel for optimal performance.
